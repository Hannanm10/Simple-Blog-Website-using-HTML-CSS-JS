World Cup History

The most recent FIFA World Cup took place in 2022 in Qatar, with Argentina emerging as the winners by defeating France on penalties in an epic final. The next World Cup is scheduled for 2026 in USA. Previuos winners include France in 2018, Germany in 2014. Spain triumphed in 2010 while Italy defeated France in 2006 to win the Fifa World Cup.

Ballon d'Or History

The Ballon dor is considered as the most prestigious individual award in football. The award is given by the France Football Federation. Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo have dominated the Ballon d'Or awards for several years. Lionel Messi holds the record for the most Ballon dors in the history of the game triumphing 8 times. He won his most recent one, eighth, in 2023 for winning the World Cup. Cristiano Ronaldo has 5 Ballon dors in his vast individual trophies cabinet.

Top Goalscorers in History

There have been many prolific goalscorers in the history of the game. The portuguese superstar Cristiano Ronaldo is the all-time highest goal scorer in the world with 869 offical goals. He is followed by the Argentine Lionel Messi with 821 official goals. The austrian Josef Bican closes the podium with 805 goals.
